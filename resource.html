Resources & Law Statutes

At InfoMineral, we believe that access to information is the foundation of informed decision-making. In today’s rapidly evolving business and social environment, understanding the laws and regulations that govern industries is as important as having accurate data

Our Resources & Law Statutes section is designed to provide businesses, institutions, and the public with free, easy-to-access summaries, full texts, and practical insights on the laws of Eswatini. This includes key statutes, regulatory frameworks, and policy guides that are relevant to economic, commercial, and social activities across the country.

Why InfoMineral Provides These Resources for Free

Empowering Citizens and Businesses

Every citizen and organization has the right to understand the laws that affect them. By providing these resources freely, we enable individuals, entrepreneurs, and institutions to make informed decisions with confidence.

Promoting Transparency and Compliance

Understanding legal requirements helps businesses stay compliant and fosters a culture of transparency. Free access ensures that knowledge is not a barrier to responsible business and civic engagement.

Bridging Data and Law

InfoMineral’s mission is to turn raw information into actionable insights. By combining legal content with practical interpretation, we help users apply the law in real-world contexts, whether for strategic planning, research, or community initiatives.

Supporting National Development

Knowledge drives progress. Making laws and resources accessible contributes to a more informed, innovative, and resilient society.
