FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about our services.

What is InfoMineral?

InfoMineral is a research and data services company that helps organizations collect, clean, and understand data. We support businesses, government institutions, NGOs, and researchers by turning raw information into clear insights that help them make better decisions.

What services does InfoMineral provide?

InfoMineral offers a range of data and research services, including: Data collection (surveys, field research, interviews), Data cleaning and organization, Market and consumer research, Digital literacy and telecom surveys, Monitoring and evaluation support, Our goal is to make data useful, reliable, and easy to understand.

Who can work with InfoMineral?

We work with many different types of clients, including: Businesses and corporations, Government departments, NGOs and development organizations, Universities and researchers, Startups and entrepreneurs, Anyone who needs reliable data can benefit from our services.

Why is data collection important?

Data helps organizations understand their customers, communities, and operations better. With accurate data, organizations can make informed decisions, improve services, and identify opportunities for growth

What makes InfoMineral different?

General FAQs

What is Data?

Data refers to pieces of information that can be collected and analyzed to understand patterns, trends, and behaviors. Organizations use data to support decision-making and improve their services or products.

Why is Data important for Organizations?

YData helps organizations make informed decisions rather than relying on assumptions. It allows businesses and institutions to understand their customers, measure performance, and identify opportunities for growth.

What is Data collection?

Data collection is the process of gathering information from people, systems, or environments. This can include surveys, interviews, observations, or digital records.

What is Data analysis?

Data analysis is the process of examining collected information to identify patterns, trends, or relationships. The goal is to transform raw data into useful insights that help organizations make better decisions.

What is Data cleaning?

Data cleaning is the process of checking and correcting errors, missing information, or inconsistencies in a dataset. Clean data improves the accuracy and reliability of research findings.

What is the difference between quantitative and qualitative data?

Quantitative data is numerical information that can be measured or counted, such as percentages, totals, or ratings. Qualitative data describes opinions, experiences, or perceptions, often collected through interviews or open-ended questions. Both types of data are valuable and often used together in research.

What is a dataset?

A dataset is a structured collection of related information, usually organized in tables or spreadsheets. Each row typically represents a record or response, while each column represents a variable or category of information.

What is data visualization?

Data visualization is the presentation of data in visual formats such as charts, graphs, and dashboards. This makes complex information easier to understand and communicate.

How is data used in business decision-making?

Organizations use data to understand market trends, customer preferences, operational performance, and risks. This helps leaders make strategic decisions based on evidence rather than guesswork.

How is personal data protected in research?

YResponsible research organizations follow ethical guidelines to protect personal information. Data is handled confidentially and used only for the purpose of the research project.
